# Assign a control tip to a Page or Tab

1. Select an individual **[Page](../../reference/user-interface-help/page-object.md)** or **[Tab](../../reference/user-interface-help/tab-object.md)**. Be sure to select an individual **Page** or **Tab**, not the corresponding **[MultiPage](../../reference/user-interface-help/multipage-control.md)** or **[TabStrip](../../reference/user-interface-help/tabstrip-control.md)**. When a **Page** or **Tab** is selected, a rectangle appears around its caption.
    
2. Right-click the selected **Page** or **Tab**.
    
3. From the shortcut menu, choose **Rename**.
    
4. In the **ControlTipText** field, type the string you want to use as the [control tip](../../Glossary/glossary-vba.md#control-tip).
    
5. Choose **OK**.
    
> [!TIP] 
> To assign a control tip for a **MultiPage** or **TabStrip**, use the **[ControlTipText](../../reference/user-interface-help/controltiptext-property.md)** property.

# XlTopBottom enumeration (Excel)

Specifies the top 10 values from the top or bottom of a series of values.



|Name|Value|Description|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **xlTop10Bottom**|0|Top 10 bottom values|
| **xlTop10Top**|1|Top 10 values|
